My Hobby

Everyone has a hobby that they enjoy doing in their free time. Hobbies help people relax, learn new skills, and spend their time in a positive way. Some people enjoy reading books, while others like playing sports, cooking, drawing, or listening to music.

Having a hobby can make people happier and healthier. It also helps them make new friends who share the same interests.

Writing Task

Write a paragraph of 60–80 words about your favorite hobby.

In your writing, you should:

  1. State what your hobby is.
  2. Explain why you like this hobby.
  3. Describe how often you do it.
  4. Mention one benefit of having this hobby.

Assessment Criteria

  • Relevance of ideas
  • Simple organization
  • Basic vocabulary use
  • Grammatical accuracy
  • Spelling and punctuation
